ABOUT ME


Mayura Vartak is a dedicated graduate student pursuing a Master of Science in Information Systems at Syracuse University with a Certificate of Advanced Studies in Data Science. With a strong academic foundation, Mayura has delved into subjects such as data analysis, natural language processing, and enterprise risk management. She combines her technical proficiency in Python, R, SQL, and tools like Tableau and Power BI with her passion for storytelling through data. Her academic achievements reflect her drive to excel in the dynamic field of data science and information systems.

Mayura brings professional experience from her tenure at Tata Consultancy Services, where she demonstrated leadership and innovation as a Systems Engineer. Managing a team of 10, she excelled in tasks like error resolution, post-production support, and scripting automation. Her notable accomplishment includes creating Python and shell scripts that reduced billing processing time from three days to five minutes, showcasing her knack for problem-solving and efficiency. Additionally, she contributed to training new hires and documenting project workflows, reflecting her collaborative and detail-oriented approach.

In addition to her professional and academic pursuits, Mayura has led impactful projects, such as designing a scalable data pipeline on Azure for real-time analytics and creating interactive Power BI dashboards to aid decision-making. Her research contributions, including co-authoring and publishing papers on fire-fighting robots and data security, further exemplify her multidisciplinary expertise and innovative mindset. With certifications like Tableau Desktop Specialist and Microsoft Office Specialist in Excel, Mayura is equipped to thrive in roles that demand analytical thinking, technical acumen, and collaborative problem-solving.
